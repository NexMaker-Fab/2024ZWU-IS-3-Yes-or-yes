Project Introduction

01 Sustainable development goals

Good health and well-being - ensuring healthy lifestyles and promoting well-being at all ages.
Specific description: To improve mental health, the project helps users identify and manage
stress. For people with autism, the program helps improve their social interaction and emotional
expression by providing emotional support and data monitoring, thereby enhancing social well-being.

02 Main function

The project designed a cuddle doll that can gauge stress levels by sensing how hard the user is
hugging.
When the user's stress level is detected to be too high, the doll automatically plays relaxing
music and provides breathing guidance to help the user relax and de-stress.

03 Project addresses problem points

The project aims to address the stress problem prevalent in modern society and provide an innovative
and interactive solution. Through the design of the hug doll, the project encourages users to pay
more attention to their emotional and stressful states in their daily lives and take proactive
steps to manage and relieve them.
The project also helps users relax more effectively and improve their quality of life by providing
features such as soothing music and breathing guidance. At the same time, for special groups such
as people with autism, the project also provides additional emotional support.

04 Target group

1- Busy professionals: Faced with work pressure and fast pace of life, professionals often feel
anxious and nervous. This cuddle doll can become a relaxing corner for them to relax, relieve work
stress and improve work efficiency and quality of life through hugging and musical breathing guidance.

2- Student population: Students facing academic pressure, test anxiety, and time management challenges,
especially high school and college students.

3- People with Autism: People with autism may face challenges in emotional expression and social
interaction. Cuddle dolls provide emotional comfort.

OPPO and ideal car cooperation case

The cooperation between OPPO and Ideal Automobile
demonstrates the application of IoT technology in
the field of intelligent vehicles. Through this
partnership, OPPO's smartphone users will be able
to leverage deep integration of their devices with
their ideal car for a range of intelligent control
functions. And not only through OPPO mobile phones,
OPPO watches are also endowed with this linkage
function.
